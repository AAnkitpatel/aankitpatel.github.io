About Me

I'm a Senior Platform / DevOps Engineer based in Noida, India, with 6+ years of experience. I've spent most of that time as the only DevOps person at W3Villa Technology, running infrastructure for 20+ production platforms in gaming, e-commerce, and enterprise SaaS.

Day to day that means AWS and GCP, Kubernetes (EKS, K3s, Cilium, ArgoCD), Terraform, and CI/CD pipelines with security scanning built in. I moved 20+ services from EC2 to ECS Fargate, which cut costs by about 40% and sped up deployments by about 85%. I also built out the observability stack (Prometheus, Grafana, New Relic) that cut our MTTR by roughly a third.

More recently I've been doing MLOps work: MLflow pipelines, containerized training jobs, FastAPI inference services on Kubernetes. I'm also picking up LLMOps and DevSecOps on the side, things like RAG pipelines, OPA/Gatekeeper, and Falco.

I hold the CKA and RHCSA certifications.

Currently exploringLLMOps (vLLM, LangChain, Qdrant), DevSecOps (Trivy, Semgrep, Cosign, Vault), Agentic AI infrastructure
